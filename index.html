About me

What I do

I help turn ideas into objects and experiences — through industrial design, CMF, 3D modelling, and digital design. I work across physical and digital, from product form to UI flow.

My background

Based in India, I'm an Associate Designer open to freelance projects and full-time roles. My practice spans industrial design, product development, CMF, and UI/UX.

My approach

Good design starts before the sketch pad — with questions about the user, the context, and what the object needs to feel like to do its job well. Honest, purposeful, beautifully made.
